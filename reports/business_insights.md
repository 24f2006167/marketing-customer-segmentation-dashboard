# Business Insights Report

## Key Findings

- Customers were successfully segmented into 5 groups.
- High-income customers generally showed higher spending behavior.
- Some customers had high income but lower spending, indicating potential upselling opportunities.
- One cluster contained outlier high-income customers.
- Customer segmentation can improve marketing personalization.

---

## Recommendations

- Focus loyalty campaigns on premium customer groups.
- Target medium-spending customers with personalized offers.
- Re-engage low-spending customers using discounts and campaigns.
- Use segmentation for targeted marketing strategies.
- Monitor outlier customers separately for strategic analysis.

---

## Business Value

This project helps businesses:
- improve customer targeting
- increase customer retention
- optimize marketing campaigns
- understand customer behavior
- improve revenue generation
